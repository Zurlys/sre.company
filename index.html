Who we are

Practical engineers,
not slide-deck consultants.

SRE.company is a boutique infrastructure and reliability engineering firm. We work directly with engineering teams and technical leadership - no account managers, no middlemen. Just engineers who get things done.

30 years of hands-on experience, starting from writing Perl and Pascal in the 90s. We've lived through every transition - sysadmin, DevOps, SRE - and earned each title the hard way. We've guided datacenter migrations that couldn't slip, diagnosed outages at 3 AM that no one else could crack, and inherited infrastructure held together by duct tape and institutional memory. That depth of hard-won knowledge means fewer surprises, faster diagnosis, and solutions built to last.

We also have a soft spot for developers. We've shipped code, felt the pressure of deploys, and know what it's like when infrastructure gets in the way of building. That shared experience shapes how we work - we're on your side.

We take on engagements ranging from short-term crisis response to long-term embedded partnerships. Our work is hands-on: we configure the systems, write the automation, and own the outcomes.

Remote-first with engineers across Central Europe and the Canary Islands. Available across EU time zones.
